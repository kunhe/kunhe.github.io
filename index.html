About

I am a research scientist in Facebook Reality Labs, working on the future of interaction tracking for Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality, using computer vision and machine learning techniques. (We are looking for interns!)

Previously, I earned PhD (2018) and MSc (2013) degrees in computer science from Boston University, advised by Professor Stan Sclaroff. My PhD dissertation focused on learning vector embeddings for image retrieval and other related computer vision tasks. My Bachelor's degree is from Zhejiang University, China. During grad school, I did research internships at Disney Research and Honda Research Institute, and I was a visiting student at Brown University.
