About

I am a research scientist in Facebook Reality Labs, working on the future of Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality. Previously, I earned PhD (2018) and MSc (2013) in computer science from Boston University, advised by Professor Stan Sclaroff. My Bachelor's degree is from Zhejiang University.

I am interested in exploring solutions to computer vision problems using machine learning techniques, and I'm frequently attracted by the optimization aspect in such problems. My PhD dissertation focused on learning vector embeddings for high-dimensional visual data, which finds application in image retrieval and other related vision tasks.

During grad school, I did research internships with Leonid Sigal at Disney Research, and with Yan Lu at Honda Research Institute. I also worked with Pedro Felzenszwalb as a visiting student at Brown University.
